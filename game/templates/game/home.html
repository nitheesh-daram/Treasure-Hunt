Jaipur brahman is a treasure hunt game where you have to reach to the location by finding a particular treasure at each place. Each place is a heritage place of Jaipur- The capital of Rajasthan.

Also known as “Pink City”. You have to find the place and treasure by using maps, QR Codes, Compass, Robot proof etc. You will be given 45 minutes to complete the treasure hunt. You will get bonus and points after every location and also leader board will be used to show your level and points and can easily compare with your friends’ level of game.

INTRODUCTION

This game is made for tourist who love to travel and know more about the places. We have included all the heritage places so you can visit the best places and know the culture of it. As the level goes up, you will have to find the place and get the treasure, you will get all the information and description of the places after reaching there. The reason behind the structure of places and reason of building will be told after playing. So, roll up your sleeves, pull your Socks!! Get ready for the game.

Rules

  1. You will get 45 minutes to complete the task.
  2. the map is embedded in the game itself. so, you cannot use other device for the help.
  3. Pay attention to details and team up properly inside the app. 
  4. Teams of 2-4 people. No more, no less. Whoever is registered on the team inside the app are the only people who qualify for prizes.
  5. You and your teammate(s) must all be registered! Register now, then pick your teammate(s) any time before the Hunt and make sure they register too!
  6. You are allowed to buy tickets for your teammates, but they must check themselves in on the app to sign and agree to Waiver, Rules, etc.
